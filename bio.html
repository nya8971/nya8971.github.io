Biography

Grammy Nominated Markus Randle better known by his stage name "Childish Major" is an Atlanta based artist/song-writer/producer. Originally from Edgefield, South Carolina, Markus has made his mark in the Atlanta music scene by working with various national artist such as Rocko, Future, SZA, J. Cole, Isaiah Rashad, Pusha T, Fall Out Boy, and Young Jeezy. Major first broke into the music industry in 2013 after producing RIAA Certified Gold selling record "U.O.E.N.O" for Atlanta Hip-Hop artist Rocko. Major is also known for his work in the Atlanta music scene, building with home-grown artist such as 6lack, Two9, Rome Fortune, EarthGang, J.I.D, Key!, as well as many others. In 2015 Markus was chosen among 8 other artist, producers, and musicians to attend the Red Bull Music Academy at Bonnaroo where they spent the duration of the festival collaborating and sharing different music making techniques. Since then Childish has dropped his debut project Woo$ah with features from 6lack, Dram, SZA,and Isaiah Rashad. With over 12 million streams Childish hit single "Supply Luh' was featured in the season 2 finale of HBO hit series "Insecure". NoEyeInTeam which is another single from the debut project “Woo$ah” was also featured in 2k19. On August 10th Childish released his first single “Know Nothing” which will be on his upcoming project “Dirt Road Diamond”. Know Nothing was featured in the first episode of season 3 of HBO hit series "Insecure".
